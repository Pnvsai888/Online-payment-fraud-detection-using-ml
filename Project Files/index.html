AI Behavioral Analysis Logic

Account Depletion (Fraud)

Transactions that leave the origin account with a perfectly zero balance are flagged as high-risk "Cash-Out" attempts.

Anomalous Velocity (Fraud)

Large, one-time transfers that exceed 200% of the account's average monthly transaction size trigger immediate review.

Micro-Payment Flow (Legit)

Small, recurring payments to known merchant IDs (utility, coffee, groceries) are classified as Legitimate behavior.

Risk Classification Framework

Probability Score Risk Assessment System Action
